Project Success Criteria

Project success will be demonstrated by achieving the following objectives:

  • Operational Hub: Fully operational and staffed Vets2PM MPLW hub in Colorado Springs by Q3 CY26.
  • Partnerships: Two formal TAP partnership agreements secured for sustained enrollment.
  • Financial Goal: First cohort starts July 2026, generating projected first-year revenue between $863K and $1M.
  • Engagement: Strong employer engagement and at least 90% satisfaction from students and partners.
